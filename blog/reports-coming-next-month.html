Reports Are Now Live in Invoiso

The Reports section is live. Revenue summaries, aged receivables, tax reports, customer statements, top products, and CSV exports — all built in.

Invoiso TeamMay 20262 min read

Reports are now available in Invoiso. Every report listed below ships in the current release — no subscription, no extra setup.

What is included

  • Revenue summary — billed, collected, outstanding, average invoice value
  • Monthly revenue trend — billed vs collected by month
  • Invoice status list — paid, partial, unpaid, overdue with CSV export
  • Aged receivables — days overdue per outstanding invoice
  • Tax report — tax collected grouped by rate
  • Top customers — ranked by billed amount
  • Customer statements — full invoice and payment history per customer
  • Top products — units sold and revenue per product
  • Currency scope — filter by active currency or view all

How to access

Open the Reports tab from the left navigation rail. Use the date range picker at the top to filter any report by period. Each report has a CSV export button for spreadsheet use.

See the full breakdown of each report type in the essential business reports guide, or download Invoiso to try them now.
