Offline-first billing

Offline Billing Software That Keeps Your Data on Your Computer

Invoiso is desktop billing software for small businesses that prefer local control. Create invoices, manage customers and products, track payments, and export PDFs without requiring a browser dashboard, cloud account, or constant internet connection.

Download Desktop AppOffline vs Cloud

Local invoice data

Your customers, products, invoices, and settings are stored locally. That makes Invoiso useful for businesses that do not want daily billing tied to a remote service.

PDF invoices offline

Create professional invoices and quotations as PDF files from the desktop app. Keep copies for customers, accounting, and backup workflows.

Backup and restore

Use backups to move or protect business data. Offline software still needs a backup habit, and Invoiso gives you that workflow inside the app.

When offline billing is a good fit

  • Billing counters with unstable internet
  • Small businesses that do not want forced logins
  • Freelancers who want invoices stored on their own machine
  • Linux users looking for desktop invoice software

When cloud billing may be better

If your team needs real-time multi-device sync, accountant access from anywhere, or mobile app workflows, a cloud product may be more suitable. Invoiso focuses on simple local billing and PDF invoice creation.
