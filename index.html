Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions

Is Invoiso completely free?

Yes — Invoiso is 100% free and open source. No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no premium tiers. Download it and use it indefinitely.

Is Invoiso free open source billing software?

Yes. Invoiso is free open source billing software for Windows, Linux, and macOS. You can use it for invoices, quotations, payments, customers, products, GST fields, PDF exports, and offline backups without paying a subscription.

Does Invoiso work without an internet connection?

Absolutely. All your data — customers, products, and invoices — is stored locally on your device. No internet connection is ever required.

Does Invoiso support GST invoicing and UPI QR codes?

Yes to both. Invoiso supports GSTIN fields, HSN codes, and per-item tax rates for GST-ready invoices. You can also add a UPI payment QR code to every PDF — customers can scan and pay via GPay, PhonePe, or Paytm instantly.

Is my data safe? Can I back it up?

Your data never leaves your device — no cloud, no remote servers. Use Settings → Backup to export a database file you can restore at any time.

Can I track payments against an invoice?

Yes. Invoiso has built-in payment tracking. Record partial or full payments against any invoice, and the app automatically calculates the outstanding balance and updates the payment status (Unpaid, Partial, or Paid). You can also download a professional PDF receipt for each payment.

Is Invoiso a free alternative to Vyapar?

Yes. Invoiso is a free, open-source alternative to Vyapar for Windows, Linux, and macOS. Unlike Vyapar, Invoiso requires no account or phone number signup, is completely free with no paid tier or subscription, and is open source under the MIT licence. Both apps support offline billing and GST-ready invoices with UPI QR codes. Invoiso also supports Linux and macOS, which Vyapar does not.

Can I use Invoiso as free GST billing software for my business in India?

Yes. Invoiso works as free GST billing software for Indian businesses. It supports GSTIN fields for your company and customers, HSN codes per product, CGST/SGST/IGST tax rates, UPI QR code payments on PDF invoices, and generates GST-compliant invoices entirely offline. No subscription required.
