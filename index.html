About Invoiso

Invoiso is a cross-platform desktop invoicing application built for simplicity and speed. Designed specifically for freelancers and small businesses, it allows you to manage customers, products, and invoices seamlessly — even without internet access.

Available for Windows and Linux, Invoiso helps you stay productive wherever you work. Your data never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy and control.

Cross-Platform Open Source No Subscription Lightweight

Invoiso is free and open source — if it saves you time, consider supporting its development.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Invoiso

Is Invoiso completely free?

Yes — Invoiso is 100% free and open source. There are no subscriptions, no hidden fees, and no premium tiers. Download it, use it indefinitely, and contribute on GitHub.

Does Invoiso work without an internet connection?

Absolutely. Invoiso is designed to work entirely offline. All your data — customers, products, and invoices — is stored locally on your device. No internet connection is required at any point.

Which operating systems does Invoiso support?

Invoiso is available for Windows and Linux. Download the latest installer for your platform from the GitHub Releases page.

Can I export invoices as PDF?

Yes. Invoiso lets you generate professional PDF invoices with a single click. Choose from three templates — Classic, Modern, and Minimal — and add your company logo and branding.

Is my business data safe with Invoiso?

Your data never leaves your device. Invoiso stores everything in a local database — no cloud sync, no remote servers. You are in full control, with a built-in backup feature to keep your data portable and protected.

Does Invoiso support multiple currencies?

Yes. Invoiso supports multiple currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, INR, JPY, AED, SGD, AUD, CAD, and JMD. You can set your preferred currency in the invoice settings.
